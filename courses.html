Class Offerings

Code Description Pricing Length
ASA 101 This is the beginning class. This class will take the beginner student from no experience to being able to pilot a 35 foot cruiser in and out of the slip, Raise, lower and trim the sails, in daylight moderate conditions. Per Group pricing. 1st student $800.00 Additional students $700.00 2 Days
ASA 103 This class is the second in the certification process. This class adds more sailing skills and practice, plus adds chart reading and navigation. VHF radio operations and additional knots are included. Additional docking sailing and sail trim practice in daylight moderate conditions are part of this class. Per Group pricing. 1st student $800.00 Additional students $700.00 (Combined with 101 class has a $100.00 discount per student in a 3 day class) 2 Days
ASA 104 This class goes in depth into the boat systems. We will examine the engine systems, (Raw water, Closed water, Fuel ) House water system, Head system, House and shore power systems. We will cover general maintenance and trouble shooting procedures. We will plan a trip to a nearby port and will travel to that port using dead reckoning procedures. Per Group pricing. 1st student $800.00 Additional students $700.00 2 Days
ASA 105 This is a take home class. No on board sailing. This class will teach more in depth navigation skills. Chart reading, tide tables and course plotting. $450.00 per Student
ASA 106 This class teaches navigation, trip planning and night time sailing in moderate conditions. This trip will be overnight to a more distant port. $1500.00 per student 3 Days
